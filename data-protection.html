Data Protection Information

Last updated: October 2024

Overview

This page provides detailed information about how Lottery of Choice handles your data, in compliance with Google Play's data protection requirements and applicable data protection laws including GDPR.

Data Controller

The data controller for Lottery of Choice is Merlin's Wizardry.

Contact: privacy@merlinswizardry.com

Types of Data Collected

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis Retention Period
Game Data (player names, round options, votes) Core app functionality for running voting rounds Legitimate interest Session-based (deleted after round completion)
Device Information App optimization and bug fixing Legitimate interest 90 days
Usage Analytics App improvement and feature development Legitimate interest 12 months

Data Processing Activities

Collection

Data is collected directly from users through the app interface and automatically through app usage.

Storage

All data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Processing

Your data is processed to provide the app's core functionality, including creating voting rounds, collecting votes from players, calculating results, and running the Final Lottery to determine winners.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

User Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR and other data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@merlinswizardry.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery
  • Report to relevant supervisory authorities as required
  • Take immediate steps to mitigate the breach
  • Provide information about the breach and protective measures

Third-Party Services

Lottery of Choice may use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns (anonymized data only)
  • Cloud Hosting: To store and process data securely

All third-party processors are carefully selected and contractually obligated to protect your data.

Cookies and Tracking

The Lottery of Choice mobile app does not use cookies. However, we may collect device identifiers for analytics purposes. You can opt out of analytics in the app settings.

International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission

Children's Data Protection

Lottery of Choice is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Data Retention Policy

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

Automated Decision-Making

Lottery of Choice does not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects or similarly significantly affects users.

Updates to Data Protection Practices

We regularly review and update our data protection practices to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations. Any material changes will be communicated to users through the app or email.

Supervisory Authority

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

Contact Information

For any questions or concerns about data protection, please contact:

Email: privacy@merlinswizardry.com
