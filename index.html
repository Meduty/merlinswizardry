Welcome to Lottery of Choice

A fast, pass-and-play party game for making group decisions—fairly, transparently, and with a little suspense at the end.

Features

  • Play & Pass: One device, many voters. Perfect for gatherings, clubs, classrooms.
  • Two Modes: Weighted or Ranked voting—choose the vibe you want.
  • Unlimited Players & Options: Scale it up or keep it cozy.
  • Transparent Math: First-past-the-post shoutout + suspenseful weighted draw.
  • Hidden Soft Caps: Gently cap inputs so play stays fast and fair.

How It Works

  • Create a Round: Enter player names and a list of options.
  • Pick a Mode: Choose Weighted (sprinkle weights) or Ranked (place tokens).
  • Pass the Device: Each player completes their ballot in turn.
  • Reveal Results: See the top line, then watch the Final Lottery crown the winner!
